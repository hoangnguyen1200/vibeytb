Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher (the "App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application and services.

1. About TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher

TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher is an automated video publishing application that discovers trending AI tools, generates short-form video reviews, and publishes them to social media platforms including YouTube and TikTok using their official APIs.

2. Information We Collect

TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher collects minimal information necessary to operate:

  • OAuth Tokens: When you authorize TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher to access your TikTok or YouTube account, we receive and store OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens. These are used solely to publish videos on your behalf.
  • Basic Profile Information: We may access your display name and account identifier as provided by the platform's API during the OAuth flow.
  • Published Content Metadata: We store metadata about videos published through TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher (e.g., video ID, publish status, timestamp) for operational tracking.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher does not collect:

  • Personal information such as email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses
  • User-generated content or private messages
  • Analytics data, browsing history, or device fingerprints
  • Financial or payment information

4. How We Use Information

Information collected by TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with social media platform APIs (YouTube, TikTok)
  • Publish video content to authorized accounts
  • Monitor publishing status and handle errors

5. Data Storage and Security

TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher stores OAuth tokens securely using environment variables and encrypted secrets management. Tokens are never hardcoded, committed to source control, or shared with third parties.

6. Third-Party Services

TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher integrates with the following third-party services:

  • TikTok Content Posting API — for publishing videos to TikTok
  • YouTube Data API v3 — for publishing videos to YouTube
  • Google Gemini API — for AI-powered content generation
  • Microsoft Edge TTS — for text-to-speech voiceover

Each service is governed by its own privacy policy. TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher does not share your data with any other third parties.

7. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained only as long as necessary for TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher to function. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok or YouTube account settings, which will invalidate the stored tokens.

8. Children's Privacy

TechHustleLabs Auto Publisher is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
